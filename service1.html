KAAL SARP DOSH PUJA IN UJJAIN

There are many well-known Brahmins who are experts in Kaal Sarp Dosh Puja in Ujjain by the Vedic method. They believe that when good deeds don't yield results, then it is worth considering.

The Kaal Sarp Dosh Puja Ujjain is a ritual performed in Hinduism to reduce the negative effects of Kaal Sarp Dosha. The Kaal Sharp Dosh is used to describe an astrological condition in which all the planets are situated between the two lunar nodes “Rahu and Ketu”. This alignment is believed to have an impact on the particular person’s life, causing challenges, obstacles, and delays.

The Kaal Sarp Dosh Pooja in Ujjain aims to reduce the celestial forces of Rahu and Ketu, and seek their blessings for mitigating the dosha's influence.

TYPES OF KAAL SARP DOSH

There are twelve types of Kaal Sarp Yog, they are :
Anant Kaal Sarp
Kulik Kaal Sarp
Vasuki Kaal Sarp
Shankhpal Kaal Sarp
Padma Kaal Sarp
Mahapadma Kaal Sarp
Takshak Kaal Sarp
Karkotak Kaal Sarp
Shankhchud Kaal Sarp
Ghaatak Kaal Sarp
Vishdhar Kaal Sarp
Sheshnag Kaal Sarp

On the basis of direction, there are two types of Kaal Sarp Yog Ascending and descending. In case, all the planets are eaten away by the Rahu’s then it is called Ascending Kaal Sarp Yog. Whereas Descending Kaal Sarp Yog is when all planets are situated in the back of Rahu. The Kaal Sarp Yog is completely effective when all the houses between Rahu and Ketu have planets.

EFFECTS OF KAAL SARP YOG

Family Disputes: There are family disputes and everyday fights among the people living in the same house. In some cases, even a friend becomes an enemy. Moreover, the natives also face some incurable diseases.

Financial Instability: The people with Kaal Sarp Yog often tend to have financial problems. The sudden loss of money and prestige is an indication of this Kaal Sarp yog.

Negative Dreams: People have negative dreams, due to Kaal Sarp Dosh. In the dreams, individuals imagine dead snakes, drowning in the river, paralyzed persons, etc.

Health-Related Issues: Due to Kaal Sarp Yog, the family suffers from diseases such as heart, eyes, and ears. Moreover, the individual having Kaal Sarp Yog will suffer from mental instability.
