MANGALDOSH PUJA IN UJJAIN

The Mangaldosh Puja in Ujjain is performed in Hinduism to reduce or neutralize the negative effects of Mangal Dosha.

Mangal Dosh occurs when the planet Mars is placed in specific houses in the birth chart. These houses are the 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, and 12th houses. If Mars is located in any of these houses in the birth chart, it creates Mangal Dosh. The intensity of the dosh varies based on whether it is present in one, two, or all of these houses in the birth chart.

The most severe form of Manglik Dosha occurs when the planet Mars is positioned in the seventh house in a person's birth chart. The intensity reduces in the following order: Mars in the 8th house, then the 4th house, and at last the 12th house.

When Mars is in these houses, it can have negative effects on various aspects of life. Additionally, the presence of the Sun, Saturn, Rahu, or Ketu in these houses can also contribute to a partial Manglik Dosha. However, Performing the Mangal Dosh Puja Ujjain helps reduce these negative effects and brings peace and harmony.

In the process of matching the horoscope, if there is a mismatch in the manglik status of the bride and groom. Then the marriage between them will be considered inappropriate.

Effects of Manglik Dosh

Manglik Dosh is considered to have a negative impact on the individual’s life, mainly in respect of marriage and relationships. The effects can be different, depending on the intensity of the dosh. Here are some of the potential effects of Manglik Dosh:

Delays or Obstacles in Marriage: The most common effect of Manglik dosha is a Delay in Marriage. It is believed that individuals with Manglik Dosha may face delays or difficulties in finding a suitable partner. Additionally, they can experience obstacles in their marital journey.

Compatibility Issues: Manglik Dosha is often associated with potential challenges in compatibility between partners. If a Manglik is married to a non-manglik, then it can lead to the event of death, conflicts, and disharmony in the relationship.

Increased Temperamental Traits: People with Manglik Dosha may exhibit stronger or more fiery temperaments, which can affect their interpersonal relationships.

Health Concerns: Some astrological beliefs suggest that Manglik Dosha can be associated with certain health issues or accidents, although this may vary on an individual basis.

The procedure of Mangal Dosh Puja Ujjain

The primary step of Bhaat Puja in Ujjain is the purification of the devotee through a ritual bath. Thereafter, The Mangalnath Puja in Ujjain is conducted by a qualified priest or astrologer. Therein, the devotee offers prayers and performs rituals to appease the planet Mars, seeking its blessings and eliminating the negative effects of Mangal Dosha.

Sacred mantras and hymns are chanted, followed by the lighting of sacred fire (homa) while offering ghee, grains, and other symbolic items. Offerings of flowers, fruits, and sweets are made to the deities associated with Mars, such as Lord Hanuman or Goddess Durga. The Mangaldosh Puja in Ujjain concludes with seeking blessings and receiving prasad (blessed offering) from the priest.

The objective of Manglik Dosh Puja in Ujjain is to seek the blessings of Mars and mitigate any potential obstacles or challenges caused by Mangal Dosha. It is believed that puja can help bring peace, harmony, and stability in relationships, especially in the context of marriage.
